## How to use the interactive quiz

**Easiest — one click from any device:**

👉 **https://fbstack.github.io/cca-exam-prep/**

That page lists every available quiz. Click any title to open it.
No account, no download, no install required. Works on phone, tablet, and desktop.

---

**If you received a link as text** (in an email, article, or social media post)
and it did not open automatically:

1. Copy the link
2. Paste it into your browser address bar (the bar at the very top of the browser)
3. Press Enter

---

**How the quiz works once you're in:**

1. Read the question and click your answer — A, B, C, or D
2. A brief explanation appears immediately showing whether you were right
3. Click **Architect explanation** for peer-level architectural reasoning
4. Click **Beginner explanation** for a plain-English walkthrough with analogies
5. Your progress is saved automatically in your browser on your device
6. Return any time — your score history is still there
